TOURIST SPOTS IN PHILIPPINES


The Philippines is a country full of beautiful sceneries, abundant white beaches, and friendly and kind-hearted people. From the beautiful coastlines to a distinctive cuisine, you will always find comfort there. Something exciting awaits whether you travel there or invest your own business in the Philippines.


Here are 3 of the recommended destinations in the Philippines.

INTRAMUROS

It is a walled city in Manila, the capital of the Philippines. It was the former city center of modern Manila and a center of political, religious and military power at the time when Manila was governed by the Spaniards.

VIGAN

It is the most-preserved example of a Spanish colonial town, and is an UNESCO World Heritage Site. Its fusion architecture found in the bahay-na-bato, the earthquake baroque churches and the Bantay Bell Tower is what attracts most tourist to this place.

EL NIDO

An environmental jewel, it is one of the most popular destinations in Palawan, aside from Puerto Princesa. Its geography and its pristine white beaches mainly attract tourists to this location.
